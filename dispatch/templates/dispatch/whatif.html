Why capacity is the primary lever
At 200 kW, the battery charges fully in ~1.8 h of the 10 h off-peak window — power is never the bottleneck above ~100 kW. PV surplus is curtailed because the battery is already at 95% SoC from overnight grid charging when solar peaks around midday. To unlock PV value a forecast-aware dispatch would be needed: skip the overnight grid charge when tomorrow's solar will suffice — a natural next step beyond this greedy policy.
